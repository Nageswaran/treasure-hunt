Mission 4: The Dutch Blueprint Code (Language & Spelling)

Exact Answer: watt

  • Explanation: 1. Wiel (1st letter = W)
    2. Accu (1st letter = A)
    3. stroom (2nd letter = t)
    4. motor (3rd letter = t).
    Combined, they spell WATT, the scientific unit of power.
  • Proof of Uniqueness: The spelling and indexing of these specific Dutch vocabulary words yield exactly one letter combination.
  • Design Notes: This tests Dutch vocabulary while incorporating a fun science easter egg.
