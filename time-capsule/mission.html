🕵️ The Mystery of the Secret Time Capsule

An old box has been discovered underground. Solve all seven puzzles to unlock it!

Puzzle 1 – Animal Observation (Easy)

Look carefully at the bird. What kind of bird is it?

  • A. Robin
  • B. Sparrow
  • C. Crow

Take the first letter of the answer.

Hint 1: This bird often lives near people.

Hint 2: It starts with S.

Puzzle 2 – Counting Stars (Easy)

How many stars are on this flag?

Take the first letter of the number.

Hint 1: Count carefully.

Hint 2: It is more than ten.

Puzzle 3 – Code Breaking (Medium)

The secret code says:

DBU

Shift every letter back by one in the alphabet.

What word appears?

Take the first letter.

Hint 1: D becomes C.

Hint 2: It's an animal.

Puzzle 4 – Map Puzzle (Medium)

Which continent is Australia in?

Take the first letter.

Hint 1: It is also the name of the country.

Hint 2: It starts with A.

Puzzle 5 – Logic Puzzle (Hard)

Three friends found clues:

  • Ava says: "The key is in the red box."
  • Ben says: "The key is not in the red box."
  • Chloe says: "Ben is wrong."

Only ONE person is telling the truth.

Is the key in the red box? Answer YES or NO.

Take the first letter.

Hint 1: Test both possibilities.

Hint 2: Only one statement can be true.

Puzzle 6 – Real World Decision (Hard)

You find an old newspaper and an empty plastic bottle near the time capsule.

Which item should go into recycling?

  • A. Plastic bottle
  • B. A shoe
  • C. A toy dinosaur

Take the first letter.

Hint 1: Think about helping the planet.

Hint 2: It starts with P.

Puzzle 7 – Grand Reveal

Arrange your seven letters in order.

They spell the name of what is hidden inside the time capsule!
