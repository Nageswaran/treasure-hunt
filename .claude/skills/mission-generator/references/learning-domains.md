# Learning Domains

Pick 4-6 of these per mission set, and rotate the combination across mission sets for the same user so the adventures don't feel samey. Each one lists a few example puzzle seeds — use these as inspiration, not a script to copy verbatim (always verify any fact-based seed with `web_search` before using it for real).

## 🇳🇱 Dutch Language
de vs het words, spelling patterns, grammar, synonyms and opposites, word categories.
*Seeds: "Is it 'de' or 'het' [woord]?" sorting puzzle; unscramble a Dutch word from letters found in clues; find the odd-one-out in a list of synonyms.*

## ➕ Mathematics
arithmetic, percentages, fractions, dates and timelines, prime numbers, number patterns, estimation, basic geometry.
*Seeds: a discount/percentage puzzle tied to a "shop" in the story; a number-pattern lock combination; calculating how many days/years between two real historical dates.*

## 🌍 Geography & World Knowledge
countries and capitals, flags, rivers and mountains, continents, time zones, distances, maps.
*Seeds: identify a country from its flag/outline; use a real map to measure a distance; figure out what time it is somewhere else in the world.*

## 🔬 Science & Nature
space, planets, animals, weather, ecosystems, the human body, scientific discoveries.
*Seeds: order the planets by a given property; identify an animal from a set of true facts; a simple weather-reading puzzle.*

## 💻 Technology & Computers
binary numbers, QR codes, internet basics, AI and robots, computer history, digital problem-solving.
*Seeds: decode a short binary number into a letter/number; explain what a QR code is, then "scan" one described in text; spot the odd device out across computer history.*

## 🔐 Cryptography & Secret Codes
Caesar cipher, Morse code, substitution ciphers, emoji codes, hidden messages, multi-step decoding.
*Seeds: a short Caesar-shifted message revealing the next clue; a Morse code snippet; an emoji-to-word substitution key.*

## 💰 Financial Literacy
money, discounts, savings, budgets, comparing prices, value and trade-offs.
*Seeds: comparing two "deals" to find the better value; a savings-goal math puzzle; spotting a hidden fee.*

## 🧭 Navigation & Orientation
compass directions, coordinates, route planning, maps, Google Maps, Street View.
*Seeds: follow compass directions from a starting point on a real map; use Street View to find a landmark described in clues; plan the shortest route between two points.*

## 🍎 Food & Health
nutrition, food labels, healthy choices, food origins.
*Seeds: read a real nutrition label and answer a question about it; figure out which country a dish originates from.*

## 🏛️ History & Civilizations
Ancient Egypt, Romans, Vikings, inventions, Dutch history, historical timelines.
*Seeds: put historical events in order on a timeline; identify an invention from clues about its inventor and era; a Dutch-history "who am I" riddle.*

## 🎨 Art & Culture
famous paintings, monuments, architecture, festivals, music.
*Seeds: identify a famous painting/monument from an image clue; match an artist to their most famous work.*

## 📰 Critical Thinking & Media Literacy
compare sources, identify official websites, verify facts, determine trustworthy information.
*Seeds: given two "claims," figure out which one a quick search confirms is true; identify which of two websites is the official/more trustworthy one for a topic.*

## 🛡️ Internet Safety & Digital Citizenship
strong passwords, phishing, privacy, scams, safe internet behaviour.
*Seeds: spot the red flags in a fake "you won a prize!" message; rank passwords from weakest to strongest and explain why.*

## 🏠 Everyday Life Skills
reading timetables, understanding weather forecasts, planning trips, measurements, following instructions.
*Seeds: read a (fictional but realistic) train timetable to answer a question; convert between units of measurement as part of a recipe or building clue.*
